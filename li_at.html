Connect LinkedIn

To use Derrick’s LinkedIn features, connect your LinkedIn account using your LinkedIn Token (li_at).

  1. Install the Derrick Token Finder Chrome Extension
  2. Open LinkedIn.com and log in
  3. Click the Derrick extension in your browser
  4. Copy your LinkedIn Token
